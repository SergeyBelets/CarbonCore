How CarbonCore Works

A simple, transparent process for registering territories and generating carbon credits.

1

Territory Registration

Landowners register their territories with detailed information about location, size, and type.

2

Expert Verification

Expert verifies territory details, including oxygen production and CO2 absorption metrics.

3

VVB Approval

VVB authority validates and approves the territory and its metrics.

4

Token Generation

Carbon tokens are automatically generated and sent to the landowner's wallet.

Our Mission

CarbonCore aims to solve several problems in traditional carbon markets: lack of transparency, absence of standardization across projects, limited access for new participants, inefficient processes with manual verification, and underutilization of modern technology.

We're building a more accessible, transparent, and efficient carbon credit ecosystem through blockchain technology and digital innovation.
